Introducing
FlexiDeposit

Introducing a game-changing feature for students: FlexiDeposit! We know that paying security deposits can be a financial strain, so we have created a smarter way to handle it. With FlexiDeposit, you can pay your hostel security deposit through us and get a portion of it back when you need it most. Whether it is for books, supplies, or just easing the start of the term, FlexiDeposit gives you financial flexibility and peace of mind, keeping more money in your pocket while you settle into student life.
